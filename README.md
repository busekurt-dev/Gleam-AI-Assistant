# Gleam 🛍️ | AI-Powered Smart Shopping Assistant Gleam is an intelligent shopping companion designed to eliminate "choice overload" and "decision fatigue" in the e-commerce world. During my AI education process, I transformed **Prompt Engineering** techniques learned into a tangible project using **Google AI Studio**. 

## 🚀 Why Gleam? Gleam aims to end the hours of research users spend finding the best product at the most affordable price: 
- **Time Efficiency:** Utilizes the analytical power of AI instead of manual price and feature comparisons.
- **Minimalist Design:** Provides a simple and helpful interface instead of getting lost in complex filters.
- **Clear Explanations:** It doesn't just recommend a product; it clearly explains *why* you should buy it.

 ## 🧠 The "Friendly Expert" Persona Gleam bridges the gap between software logic and human communication: 
 - **A Helpful Friend:** Communicates in a sincere, supportive, and effortless tone.
 - **Experienced Consultant:** Backed by artificial intelligence, it possesses the technical depth of a senior expert in a technology store.

## 🛠️ Key Features & Logic 
- **Smart Budget Analysis:** When a "low-budget" request is made, it filters the products with the best features in that specific price range.
- **Confidence Score:** Each recommendation features a **Confidence Score** in the top-right corner, allowing users to know exactly how much they can trust the suggestion.
- **Modern AI Approach:** Beyond traditional coding patterns, it demonstrates the logic of product development by "training" and "guiding" AI (Prompt Engineering).

## 📈 Growth & Achievements I developed this project during my first year of Software Engineering to gain the following competencies: 
- **Google AI Studio Mastery:** Configuring and testing AI models.
- **Prompt Engineering:** Writing precise instructions to get the desired output from AI (Prompt Design).
- **Product Logic Design:** Building a user-centric working principle from scratch.

## 🔮 Future Plans
